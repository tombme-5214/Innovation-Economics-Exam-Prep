1. Why Innovation Matters

Innovation explains >50% historical growth via: (1) enhanced physical capital; (2) labour complementarities; (3) human capital productivity; (4) endogenous investments. OECD: "science/tech/innovation drives long-term growth". Units: individuals/firms/industries/regions/countries.

2. Definitions (Oslo Manual + More)

Oslo Manual: (1) New product/change; (2) process new to industry; (3) new market; (4) new supply; (5) org changes. Types: Product/Process/Soft (org/strategic).

4. Neoclassical vs Modern + Process

Neoclassical: exogenous "manna", codified knowledge. Modern: endogenous, non-linear process (invention‚Üíinnovation‚Üídiffusion w/ spillovers). Scientists (public good) ‚Üí firms (private R&D/IP).
